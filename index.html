Guidelines

- Items presented at MicroHack should be made during MicroHack.
- Prior planning/prototyping work is allowed and encouraged, but the goal is to see what you can make in two hours
- 3rd party libraries are allowed, but don’t claim credit for something that isn’t yours.

Judging

Judging will be a little different than at other hackathons. Instead of a 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners, there will be 3 winning teams in 3 categories:

- Technical Difficulty - How much skill is required to create your product?
- Usefulness/Practicality - Does your product help make someone's life better?
- Wildcard - How unique or extraordinary is your product?
